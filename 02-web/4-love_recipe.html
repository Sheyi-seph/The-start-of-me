How to make semovita pap

ingredients

1 and half cup semovita

3 cups of water

Half cup of milk(hollandia evap or tin milk)

Sugar to taste

Cooking instructions

Add water into a clean pot on heat & bring to boil. Then add water to semovita & make a paste, then add in th semovita paste into the boiling water & stir continuously until it begins to form bubbles & thickens.

Then off heat & mix water with milk & add into the pap. Mix well add sugar to your taste & mix, add in the flavor stir well & serve. I topped mine with some slices of Banana & grated coconut
